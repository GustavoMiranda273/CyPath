WHAT DO THESE MEAN?
●
Fitness (CTL)
Your long-term training load — a 42-day exponentially weighted average. Builds slowly and represents your aerobic capacity.
●
Fatigue (ATL)
Your short-term load — a 7-day average. This responds quickly to recent hard sessions and recovery.
●
Form (TSB)
Fitness minus fatigue. Positive numbers mean you're fresh; negative numbers mean you're carrying load. Race-day target is typically +5 to +15.
⚠️ Banister-model values are estimates derived from your planned and logged training stress, not a substitute for professional coaching or medical advice.
